After [CalAction](https://calaction.mijndertstuij.nl) I wanted to experiment a bit with [SpriteKit](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/spritekit) and try to make a small game for iPhone. I don't usually play any games on my iPhone myself, because most are overrun with garbage like ads and loot boxes.

With [Burst](https://burst.mijndertstuij.nl/) I wanted to do things differently. The bubble shooter genre (if you can call it that) has a lot of games in the App Store currently, but again most are either overrun with ads and scammy in-app purchases, or they haven't been maintained in many years.

Burst has zero ads, doesn't track you in any way, and certainly doesn't have any in-app purchases. I nice alternative to doomscrolling and apps that try to sell you something at every turn.

There's a couple of features and things included:

- **Power-ups**: bomb, lightning and rainbow power-ups earned by chaining together combos
- **Six coding-inspired themes** like Catppuccin, Dracula and Gruvbox
- **Game Center** integration for leaderboards and **iCloud sync** to keep your high score secure
- **Boast about your high score** with an image you can share in your messaging app or on social media
- **Accessibility** features like colorblind mode, VoiceOver, reduce motion and dynamic type
- Again, it's completely **free to play**, I'm never going to sell you something here

[Burst is now live on the App Store](https://burst.mijndertstuij.nl/).

Give it a try, I'd love to hear your feedback.
