**The best thing that happened this week**

This will be a long update because quite a few things happened!

The best thing that happened this week is that I made another side project public this week. [I created a thing](https://mijndertstuij.nl/posts/introducing-rss-fulltext/) that takes an RSS feed, goes through each link in the feed, downloads the article, and generates a new RSS feed including the full article. I mostly wanted this to exist for myself, but I decided to open source the thing because I think it's useful for other people as well.

That's three side projects so far in 2026!

- An iOS app called [CalAction](https://apps.apple.com/nl/app/calaction/id6760361082) to help importing .ICS files from anywhere;
- A [Terraform module](https://github.com/mijndert/aws-scp-catalog) for deploying AWS SCP's more easily;
- [RSS FullText](https://github.com/mijndert/rss-fulltext) to create full article RSS feeds.

One of my goals for 2026 was to consume less and create more. So far I'd say I'm on the right track.

Especially since the most popular alternative, Codeberg, also has frequent downtime (mostly at no fault of their own).
